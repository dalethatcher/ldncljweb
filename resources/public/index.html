The London Clojure Community
Sign up to our mailing list or follow us on Twitter!

We usually have at least three events per month, two dojo sessions and one talk. The Dojo events are fun friendly sessions for all levels to enjoy a bit of clojure, no experience required!

We're very grateful to Forward, Skills Matter and ThoughtWorks for sponsoring these events.

Sign up information is posted to our mailing list, this website and our group google calendar.

Please see below for the normal locations and timings of these events, any changes will be announced on the mailing list.
